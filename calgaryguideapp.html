The Calgary Guide App

Unfortunately, due to recent changes with the App Store terms of service, the iOS version of the Calgary Guide app is not permitted to display any content pertaining to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. This means that these slides will be inaccessible in the iOS version of the app for the time being. The Android version of the app continues to contain the complete Calgary Guide content.

Created by Dr. Yan Yu, The Calgary Guide is an internationally respected medical learning reference. A mobile app, developed by David Olmstead, is now available to the public.
